Overview

In this module, you’ll be introduced to some basics of the research process. You’ll learn about the development of a research question or objective and a research hypothesis. You’ll also learn some key similarities and differences between types of research.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify the observation that underpins a research question,
  • Assess a research question for clarity and focus,
  • Distinguish between operational and non-operational variables,
  • Describe the advantages of performing a thorough literature review,
  • List the characteristics of good research,
  • Distinguish between qualitative and quantitative research approaches and explain the reasons a researcher might select one method over the other,
  • Identify the advantages of mixed-method approaches.
