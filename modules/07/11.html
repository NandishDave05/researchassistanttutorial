As a research assistant, or even as a student, you will probably be asked to use the spreadsheet application Microsoft Excel and should be prepared to do so. To learn how to use Excel 2010 and create your first spreadsheet, click the link above and hit the Start this course button. Watch each of the seven videos, and be sure to complete the ‘Test yourself’ quiz at the end of the course!

Depending on the project you are working on, you may also be asked to use other software packages, such as IBM SPSS Statistics or NVivo. If your supervisor has told you that you will be using either of these, please review the tutorials below (whichever are appropriate for your project):

NVivo: Watch this YouTube Video to get started with NVivo!

For other helpful NVivo tutorials on working with audio files, survey results, interview data, codes, themes and more, click here!

SPSS

SPSS: Explore the SPSS Guide here, and read about the topics that you need to know for your project.

Alternatively, you can run the IBM SPSS Statistics 21 tutorial by opening the program on your computer, selecting the ‘Run the tutorial option,’ and clicking OK. The tutorial will open in your default browser, and you can choose which topics you’d like to explore. We strongly recommend starting with the ‘Introduction,’ ‘Reading Data,’ and ‘Using the Data Editor’ tutorials and continuing on with other tutorials as needed.
