Survey Question Activity

Take a look at these sample questions below that have been used in actual Humber survey research. See if you can detect the type of question that is being asked. Is it nominal, ordinal, or interval?

Question 1 : Did you come to Humber straight from high school? (Yes/No)

Question 2 :How often do you access travel websites for news and information relevant to the travel industry? (Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Every few months/Yearly/Never)

Question 3: Which of the following best describes how you most frequently access news and information on issues relevant to the travel industry in Canada? (Print Publications/Online Sources/Both Print and Online Equally)

Question 4: When working on an assignment that requires you to use supplementary resources, how often do you use print books? (Never/Rarely/Occasionally/Frequently/Always)

Question 5: On a scale of 1 to 10, rate the overall amount of stress you experienced during your field internship, with 1 being no stress at all and 10 being the most stressful experience of your life.

Question 6: What is your gender? (Male/Female)

Question 7: If you schedule group face-to-face meetings, how often do you meet? (Once or more times a day/once a week/two to three times a week/more than three times a week/once a month/other)

Question 8: What is your current Grade Point Average (GPA)? (under 60%/60-64%/65-69%/0-74%/75-79%/80-84%/over 85%)

Question 9: How often do you read the e-newsletters that you receive? (All the time/Regularly/Sometimes/Rarely/Never)
