LITERATURE REVIEW: ensures that the research project builds on all the available and credible information known about a topic.

A lit review ensures that the research project builds on all the available and credible information known about a given topic. It can also encourage researchers to find a piece of “uncharted territory” to explore. This is sometimes called a “gap” in the research, and many researchers are most interested in testing or learning about something brand new. Others recognize the value in repeating existing experiments to add to the strength of the collective findings. For example, guaranteed there has been more than one study linking smoking to cancer!
