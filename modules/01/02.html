RESearch ASSISTANT

Introduction

 

 

Research helps us to make decisions. You make hundreds, maybe thousands, of decisions everyday. You decide what clothes to wear and what first morning greeting to say to your roommates. You decide what time to wake up and which breakfast foods to eat, once flavour, availability, cost, and nutritional value have been perhaps fleetingly considered. It is likely that you make hundreds of decisions before you even leave the house for school or work!
