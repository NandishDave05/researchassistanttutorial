Overview

Upon completion, you will be granted a certificate that you must upload under the "Module 3 - Activities" in order to move on to the next module. Learning Objectives

  • Identify some possible risks or harm inherent in project design
  • Sketch a general picture of the governance and management of research ethics guidelines
  • Describe the three principles underpinning the Tri-Council Policy Statement and apply these principles in research design and data collection
  • Recognize the markers of informed consent and the implications of using deception in a research project
  • Name some special considerations when research projects include vulnerable populations
  • Distinguish between privacy and confidentiality
