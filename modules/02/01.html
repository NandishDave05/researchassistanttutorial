Overview

In this module, you’ll be introduced to the basics of the legal matters and business practices in research. You’ll learn about legal agreements, intellectual property and standards of behavior when working with academic and industry collaborators. You’ll gain an understanding of why protecting everyone’s interests – the research team’s, the partner’s, the college’s or university’s – is central to the integrity of the research process.

Learning Objectives

At the completion of this module, you will be able to:

  • Identify appropriate business practices when working with research partners
  • Distinguish different types of intellectual property created in the research process
  • Describe potential conflicts of interest for individuals, teams and nizations involved in research projects
  • Identify the main legal documents that need to be in place
  • Identify your rights and responsibilities as a student researcher
