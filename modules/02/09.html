Summary & Next Steps

In this module, we introduced the concepts of business etiquette and intellectual property within the context of applied research. You read about typical partnerships involved in applied research at colleges, the research team, the six kinds of IP protected in Canada, contracts and importantly, your rights and responsibilities as a student researcher.

Take the time to clarify any concepts or documents you are unsure about so that you can start contributing on the project with confidence.
