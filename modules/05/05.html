Literature Reviews

Summary & Next Steps

Literature reviews are an important step in the research process. When you are directing your own research, you will likely have to write one. As a research assistant, you may be asked to assist your research supervisor by gathering relevant sources (he or she may choose to write the lit review). Your job will be to make sure that you leaveÂ no stone unturnedÂ in your research period. The more familiar you can be with the search tool at your disposal, the easier it will be to locate relevant sources.

Writing a lit review is a very important research skill. Lit reviews help create a foundation of expertise that will guide the next steps of the research project. Researchers can learn from others some best practices in data collection and analysis, both of which will be discussed in the next modules.
